## Our Mission

We're a local chapter of the [Strong Towns](https://www.strongtowns.org/about) movement, whose mission is to replace our post-war pattern of development, the Suburban Experiment, with a pattern of development that is financially strong and resilient. We are focused on making Ottawa, livable, and inviting. 
 working to make Ottawa more financially resilient, livable, and safe for everyone. We believe in building a city that offers freedom and choice in how people live, work, and move.

Strong SacTown is a local chapter of Strong Towns, a national organization

  <strong>Officially incorporated as Ottawa-Gatineau Urbanism Association Inc. in November 2025</strong>, we continue our advocacy as Strong Towns Ottawa‚Äîthe community name you know us by.

## How We Create Change

We focus on practical, achievable improvements that make a real difference in our communities:

### üöå **Better Transportation**
- Advocating for bus lanes on Bank Street
- Supporting safer pedestrian facilities
- Promoting efficient transit with practical solutions

### üè° **More Housing Options**
- Encouraging diverse housing types
- Supporting mixed-use neighborhoods
- Making communities more walkable

### üë• **Community Engagement**
- Hosting urbanist book clubs and discussions
- Mobilizing members for consultations and surveys
- Connecting people with local community associations

Our approach is simple: **take the next smallest step** that moves Ottawa in the right direction. These incremental changes‚Äîlike safer sidewalks, traffic-calmed streets, and painted bus lanes‚Äîrequire minimal investment but deliver immediate benefits.
